Random Forest Machine Learning

Description

I started with 1460 records comprising of 80 Features. During the exploration process I discovered multiple categorical features with missing values. These features with missing values were removed. Next the features were greatly reduced by selecting only those with a correlation to Sale Price greater than 0.5.

The data was randomly divided into training and testing sets. I then used Random Forest Regression with RandomizedSearchCV to find the best parameters for the model.

The training data has a score of 0.978 and the testing data had a score of 0.867 with a mean square error of 729555657.23!

Following the RandomizedSearchCV the training data has a score of 0.917 and a testing score of 0.883 with a mean square error of 654125368.41!
